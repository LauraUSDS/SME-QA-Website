Choosing SMEs

To assess an estimated 100 applicants, you’ll need around eight SMEs, assuming you’ll assign one SME to conduct each phone interview. To choose SMEs, work with the hiring manager to find the best-suited SMEs to participate in your hiring action, who are also not interested in applying for the position. Potential SMEs should complete a background information form and submit a resume as part of the selection process. Document this information in the case file for the hiring action.
