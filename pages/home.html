The SME-QA Process

During a typical competitive delegated examining hiring process, human resource (HR) specialists review resumes and occupational questionnaires to determine whether applicants are minimally qualified for a role. Agencies often conduct outreach and recruitment for technical positions and are concerned when they receive certificates without qualified applicants to select from.

With the SME-QA process, subject matter experts (SMEs) partner with HR specialists to create and conduct interview assessments before considering an applicant qualified and applying veteransâ€™ preference. SME-QA is recommended for GS-12+ technical positions (not limited to IT) with at least five vacancies for the same role where applicant volume can be limited to approximately 100 applicants.
